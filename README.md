# AI-Driven Identification and Targeted Financial Service Promotion Platform

## Overview

An **AI-powered platform** designed to enhance **financial and insurance service delivery for the Department of Posts**.  
The system identifies **region-specific financial needs** using demographic, economic, and agricultural cycle data, enabling **targeted promotion of postal financial schemes** to improve **customer satisfaction, outreach effectiveness, and operational efficiency**.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Demographics & Administration Dashboards
- Multi-level population analysis:
  - State
  - District
  - Sub-Post Office
  - Branch Post Office
  - Village
- Visual, data-driven insights to tailor financial product promotions based on **local demographic realities**.

---

### 2. Scheme Timeline (Annual Planning)
- **Month-by-month recommendations** of the most relevant financial schemes.
- Recommendations aligned with:
  - Regional demographics
  - Economic activity cycles
  - Seasonal trends
- Enables **full-year promotion planning** and optimized resource allocation.

---

### 3. Public Information Table
Comprehensive individual-level profiles including:
- Personal financial information
- **Top 3 eligible financial services**
- Enrollment status:
  - Enrolled
  - Not Enrolled

---

### 4. Individual Financial Dashboard
A single-person dashboard displaying:
- Financial history
- Scheme eligibility
- Engagement with postal financial and insurance products

---

### 5. Event Calendar Integration
- Automated **web scraping** of local events using:
  - Selenium
  - BeautifulSoup
- Monthly event discovery and synchronization with **Google Calendar**
- Supports efficient planning of **Financial Promotion Melas**
- Ensures promotions align with:
  - Local events
  - Seasonal activities
  - Agricultural cycles

---

### 6. Mela Feedback Collection
- Digital feedback forms for post-event data collection
- Captures participant experience and satisfaction
- Enables **data-driven improvements** in event planning and execution

---

### 7. Task Assignment, Scheduling & Gamification
- Promotion targets assigned to:
  - Sub-Post Offices
  - Branch Post Offices
- Gamification features:
  - Points
  - Badges
  - Ranks
- **Leaderboard** to track task completion
- Geo-tagged photographs uploaded as proof of promotion activity
- Motivates field staff and improves outreach performance

---

### 8. Post-Mela Statistics & Analytics
- Detailed post-event analytics at **branch level**
- Measures:
  - Reach
  - Effectiveness
  - Enrollment impact
- Insights used to refine future strategies

---

### 9. Chatbot Assistance
- AI-powered chatbot for:
  - Field workers
  - Customers
- Answers queries related to:
  - Financial products
  - Eligibility
  - Procedures and documentation

---

### 10. Farmer Risk Analysis
- AI-driven analysis based on:
  - Agricultural cycles
  - Crop patterns
  - Seasonal risk profiles
- Tailors:
  - Insurance products
  - Savings schemes
- Ensures farmers receive **season-appropriate and risk-aware financial offerings**

---
